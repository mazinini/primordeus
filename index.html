In lumine caeli, eligitur fortis — not by birthright, nor by name, but by the fire you carry within. Primordeus Zodiac, nestled on the majestic Mount Olympus in Greece, exudes an aura of ancient mystique and divine energy. The castle’s grandeur and splendor reflect the rich history of the gods and demigods who once roamed its halls. As the sun sets behind the mountains, the castle’s walls glow with a golden hue, casting a magical ambiance over the surrounding landscape. Primordeus stands as a beacon of knowledge and power, a place where the legacy of gods and heroes continues to thrive. Ascend, children of the heavens. Your calling awaits.
